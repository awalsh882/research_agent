DocuSign (DOCU)

Equity Research Report

Report Date: January 03, 2026

Executive Summary

DocuSign is the leading e-signature platform facing competitive headwinds from bundled solutions.

Key Highlights

  • NRR declining to mid-90s
  • CLM expansion strategy critical
  • Strong brand recognition

Financial Analysis

Revenue growth has decelerated from double-digits to single-digits as post-pandemic tailwinds fade.

Business Analysis

Core e-signature market is commoditizing. Differentiation must come from CLM and AI features.

Valuation Analysis

Trading at 4-5x NTM revenue, reasonable for growth profile but requires execution on CLM.

Key Risks

1. Risk 1

Microsoft and Adobe bundling e-signature into their suites.

2. Risk 2

Must prove CLM can drive meaningful revenue growth.

Key Takeaways

  • Monitor CLM traction
  • Watch NRR stabilization
  • Evaluate competitive win rates
